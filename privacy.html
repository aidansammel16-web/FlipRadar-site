Privacy Policy

FlipRadar is a Chrome extension that helps users monitor Facebook Marketplace listings.

Data Collection

FlipRadar does not collect or store personal information such as names, email addresses, or passwords.

The extension may process and store the following information:

  • User-configured Facebook Marketplace search URLs
  • User-defined keywords and filters
  • A randomly generated device identifier
  • Subscription status (Free or Pro)

How Data Is Used

This data is used solely to provide core functionality of the extension, including matching listings and managing subscription access.

Data Sharing

FlipRadar does not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.

Payments

Payments are securely processed by Stripe. FlipRadar does not store or have access to payment information.

Permissions

The extension interacts with Facebook Marketplace pages to identify listings that match user-defined criteria. No unrelated browsing data is collected or stored.
