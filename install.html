      <section class="guide-card">

        <h2>1. Install FlipRadar</h2>

        <ol>

          <li>Open the FlipRadar listing in the Chrome Web Store.</li>

          <li>Click <strong>Add to Chrome</strong>.</li>

          <li>Confirm the installation when Chrome asks.</li>

          <li>Pin FlipRadar to your browser toolbar so it is easy to access.</li>

        </ol>


        <div class="note-box">

          After installing, click the FlipRadar icon in your browser toolbar to open the popup.

        </div>

      </section>


      <section class="guide-card">

        <h2>2. Add Your First Marketplace Search</h2>

        <ol>

          <li>Go to Facebook Marketplace.</li>

          <li>Search for the kind of product you want to monitor.</li>

          <li>Apply any Marketplace filters you want.</li>

          <li>Copy the full URL from your browser address bar.</li>

          <li>Open FlipRadar.</li>

          <li>Paste the Marketplace URL into the search field.</li>

          <li>Optionally set a max price.</li>

          <li>If you are on Pro, you can also enter keywords.</li>

          <li>Click <strong>Save Search</strong>.</li>

        </ol>


        <div class="note-box">

          Free users can monitor 1 search with 5 minute checks. Pro users get unlimited searches, keywords, and faster alerts.

        </div>

      </section>


      <section class="guide-card">

        <h2>3. Configure Your Settings</h2>


        <h3>Minimum score</h3>

        <p>This helps FlipRadar decide which listings are most relevant.</p>


        <h3>Check interval</h3>

        <p>This determines how often FlipRadar refreshes your saved searches.</p>


        <ul>

          <li><strong>Free:</strong> 5 minute minimum</li>

          <li><strong>Pro:</strong> faster checks</li>

        </ul>

      </section>


      <section class="guide-card">

        <h2>4. Set Up Telegram Alerts (Optional)</h2>


        <p>

          FlipRadar can send alerts through your own Telegram bot. You do not need Telegram to use FlipRadar, but it is useful if you want mobile notifications.

        </p>


        <h3>Step A — create a Telegram bot</h3>

        <ol>

          <li>Open Telegram.</li>

          <li>Search for <strong>BotFather</strong>.</li>

          <li>Start a chat with BotFather.</li>

          <li>Send this command:</li>

        </ol>


        <div class="code-box">/newbot</div>


        <ol start="5">

          <li>Follow the prompts to choose a bot name and username.</li>

          <li>BotFather will give you a bot token.</li>

          <li>Copy and save that token somewhere safe.</li>

        </ol>


        <h3>Step B — get your chat ID</h3>

        <ol>

          <li>Send a message to your new bot in Telegram.</li>

          <li>Open this URL in your browser, replacing <strong>YOUR_BOT_TOKEN</strong> with your real token:</li>

        </ol>


        <div class="code-box">https://api.telegram.org/botYOUR_BOT_TOKEN/getUpdates</div>


        <ol start="3">

          <li>Find the number listed under <strong>chat</strong> → <strong>id</strong>.</li>

          <li>Copy that number. That is your Telegram chat ID.</li>

        </ol>


        <h3>Step C — add both values to FlipRadar</h3>

        <ol>

          <li>Open FlipRadar.</li>

          <li>Open full settings.</li>

          <li>Paste in your Telegram bot token.</li>

          <li>Paste in your Telegram chat ID.</li>

          <li>Save your settings.</li>

        </ol>


        <div class="warning-box">

          Keep your Telegram bot token private. Anyone with that token can interact with your bot.

        </div>

      </section>

        <h2>5. How to Use FlipRadar Effectively</h2>

        <ul>

          <li>Use very specific Marketplace searches for the best results.</li>

          <li>Set a max price so you only see real opportunities.</li>

          <li>Use keywords on Pro to narrow down listings even further.</li>

          <li>Keep FlipRadar pinned so you can quickly update searches.</li>

          <li>Check alerts quickly because good Marketplace deals disappear fast.</li>

        </ul>
